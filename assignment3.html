Plot

Nine noble families fight for control over the lands of Westeros, while an ancient enemy returns after being dormant for millennia.

A popular misconception developed among viewers of the show that the show-runners David Benioff and D.B. Weiss had rushed the making of the final season so that they could move on to make a Star Wars project for Disney. While they did have several Star Wars movies planned at the time of the airing of Season 8, Benioff and Weiss have stated that their plans for the remainder of Game of Thrones (ending with Season 8) were already mapped out in 2013, long before Disney approached them.
